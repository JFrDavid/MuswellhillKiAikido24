Sensei Mark Greenley

Sensei Mark Greenley started practicing Ki Aikido in 1981 when he joined the Plymouth and Torquay clubs in Devon. Over the next few years he attended many training courses under the direction of Sensei Ken Williams, head of the Ki Federation of Great Britain at the Federations headquarters in Somerset. ki federation of great Britain

In 1984 Mark passed his 1st Dan Black belt grading and in December of that year he moved to London. He continued training at several North London clubs and travelled back to Somerset on a regular basses to attend training courses at headquarters.

In 2010 Mark was awarded 7th Dan.

He is also a grading officer for the Ki Federation and continues to practice at at headquarters attending many training courses throughout the year.

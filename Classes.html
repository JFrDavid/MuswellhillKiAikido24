wear any loose, comfortable, and clean clothing.

Your first Ki Aikido lesson will likely focus on basic concepts and movements to introduce you to the art. Ki Aikido emphasizes harmony, relaxation, and the flow of energy ("Ki"), rather than brute strength. Here's what you can expect: 1. Introduction to Ki Principles, 2. Posture and Movement, 3. Simple Techniques, 4. Respect and Etiquette. It's a chance to learn the core ideas of Ki Aikido in a calm, supportive environment.

Congratulation, you are starting your journey of learning the art of Ki Aikido. The Ki federation of Geat Britain has put in place a very well structured progression of KI exercises and Aikido techniques. You will start from the basic 1st technic (IKKIO)-1st forme to the 9th technic (KOTEGEASHI)-16th form.
